Braves Awards

The Atlanta Braves, a storied franchise in Major League Baseball, have a rich history of accolades that highlight the talent and achievements of their players and management. Notably, Ronald Acuña Jr. has been a standout, securing the MVP award in 2023, adding to the Braves’ legacy of MVPs like Hank Aaron and Chipper Jones. The team’s pitching accomplishments is evident with multiple Cy Young Awards, with legends like Greg Maddux and Tom Glavine being honored in the '90s.

In recent years, Michael Harris II emerged as the Rookie of the Year in 2022, showcasing the Braves’ ability to nurture young talent. The managerial skills of Brian Snitker earned him the Manager of the Year award in 2018, a testament to his leadership. The Braves’ offensive strength is also recognized with numerous Silver Slugger Awards, with players like Matt Olson and Austin Riley winning in 2023.

Defensively, the Braves have been just as impressive, with Max Fried and Dansby Swanson winning Gold Gloves in 2022 for their exceptional fielding abilities. The team’s commitment to community and sportsmanship is exemplified by John Smoltz’s Roberto Clemente Award in 2005.

Overall, the Atlanta Braves’ award winners reflect a franchise committed to excellence, with a blend of seasoned veterans and promising newcomers leading the way to success on and off the field.
